About us

VOMA (Art of staying alive) military-patriotic NGO was founded 2014 by Vova Vartanov. The goal of VOMA is to create an efficient reserve force. According to the VOMA ideology, Armenia must become a technological Country-Fortress, be ready for any confrontation, obstacles, be motivated and developed, having a Nation-Army.

#Country-Fortress concept

According to the ideology of VOMA, every settlement in Armenia must be capable of self-defense and armed, in order to achieve all that, we have a number of tasks:
  1. There should be shelters, firing points, fortresses all over Armenia.
  2. Great attention should be paid to the military industry.
  3. Every region of Armenia must be able to defend itself, which can be achieved through armament and technical readiness.
  4. The majority of the population must be armed in order to mobilize more quickly and easily.
  5. Schoolchildren must prepare for service.
  6. In a country where boys and girls without sex discrimination must be efficient.
  7. The army must be closely connected with the IT sector.

#Nation-Army concept



Our goal is to have between 100,000 and 600,000 combat-ready civilians to serve as a reserve army during hostilities, as well as to make up for regular army losses and to carry out counterattacks. To achieve all this, VOMA branches are opened in different communities and settlements of the country. In 2021, a branch of VOMA was opened in Vanadzor. During this period, VOMA-Vanadzor has conducted, continues to conduct quarterly, ten-day gatherings, campaigns, classroom and ABO classes. Following the Azg-Banak, Country-Fortress concept, VOMA-Vanadzor is taking strong steps to create an efficient reserve force in Vanadzor and the surrounding areas.
